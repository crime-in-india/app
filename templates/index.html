Crime in India

Opening up India's crime data

Crime in India is an effort to make India's crime records more meaningful and easier to access. We have the most updated data from the National Crime Records Bureau — 2010 to 2014. Know your city and its crime history. Keep track of different kinds of crimes.

Click on a city on the map or choose from the menu to get started. Look at crimes in different categories. Or you can simply read our stories.

Crimes

Choose a category of crime
